Frequently Asked Questions

What is Export Leadership Forum?

The Export Leadership Forum will bring together local and global leaders from across all exporting industries to discuss the latest innovations and best practices in their fields. The Forum will provide attendees with a mix of “how to” sessions, use cases to replicate, advice on avoiding pitfalls and invaluable networking opportunities.

When is Export Leadership Forum?

Export Leadership Forum will take place on 10th and 11th February 2016.

Where is the conference?

The conference will be held at Croke Park. For more information please see the venue page.

How do I get tickets?

You can purchase tickets now. The two for one discount ends on 25th October so don't delay.

What does my ticket entitle me to?

Your ticket entitles you to entry into the Export Leadership Conference as well as access to the networking drinks.

What are the networking drinks?

Networking is one of the key benefits of Export Leadership Forum. Up to 800 senior executives across a range of export industries will be together, the business benefits are invaluable. On Tuesday 6th there will be a networking drinks party held in a location to be announced in October.

Is there an agenda available?

The full agenda will be released closer to the date. In the meantime you can read all about the different streams here.

Unfortuantely we cannot pay for travel expenses for the Export Leadership Forum.
